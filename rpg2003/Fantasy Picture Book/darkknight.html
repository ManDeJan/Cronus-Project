Dark Knight

Height: 2 meters Weight: 175 lbs.
Habitat: Town Origin: ???
Meaning: English "dark knight"

The Dark Knight has its own twisted form of respect for status and justice, but much different from the "white knights" whose valor bards sung in medieval times. Most magic, especially magic that kills instantly, has very little effect. Often, dark knights are merely those who pursue strength at all costs, with no concern for the sanctity of life.
