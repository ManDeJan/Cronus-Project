Demon

Height: ??? Weight: ???
Habitat: The Underworld Origin: Europe, Greece
Meaning: Greek word "Daimonion" or "soul"

Demons that live in the underworld. Prior to the spread of Christianity, "demon" did not have a negative connotation. "Demons" were often revered by the local populace. Christianity changed the connotation to mean "a being that is hostile to God", or more specifically, a servant of Lucifer, the fallen angel.
