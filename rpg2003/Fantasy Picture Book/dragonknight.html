Dragon Knight

Height: 2 meters Weight: 400 lbs.
Habitat: ??? Origin: ???
Meaning: English "Dragon Knight"

Translated literally, it means "dragon horseman". Dragon knights are able to handle aerial enemies with ease using their dragon's ability to fly and their long spears. In some games, dragon knights take on some features of the dragons themselves, such as the ability to breathe fire. By the way, one who slays dragons is usually called a dragonslayer.
