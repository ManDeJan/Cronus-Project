Elf

Height: 1.5 to 2 meters Weight: 90-180 lbs.
Habitat: Forests and plains Origin: Europe
Meaning: "Fairy" or "Spirit"

Originally, the elf was viewed as a mischievious fairy, but evolved over time. Gradually, the elf as described in folktales assumed the form described today: humanlike with a tall frame and pointed ears. Highly intelligent, and proficient with magic and a bow, elves can make formidable opponents or allies.
