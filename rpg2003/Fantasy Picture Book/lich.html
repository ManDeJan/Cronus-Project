Lich

Height: About 2 meters Weight: About 200 lbs.
Habitat: Darkness Origin: ???
Meaning: Old English "corpse"

The walking corpses of necromancers who sought eternal life and transferred their souls to undead bodies. Also called undead mages, these men traded their humanity for immortality. Even in its semi-rotten state, the lich retains many of the powers it had in life while also mastering the ability to drain the life and memories from its victims.
