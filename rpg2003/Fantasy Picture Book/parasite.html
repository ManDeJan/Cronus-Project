Parasite

Height: 10 nanometers Weight: ???
Habitat: Host organism Origin: ???
Meaning: Greek word "parasite"

Parasites are usually microscopically small, but in RPGs, parasites are usually drawn about the size of a small animal. It usually drains one's health and MP. In general, parasites invade a host organism, siphoning nutrients from the body and causing illness. It typically enters its host through food and the like. There are about 120 kinds of parasites that inhabit human hosts.
