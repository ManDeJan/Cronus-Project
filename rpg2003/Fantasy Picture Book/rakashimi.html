Rakashimi

Height: ??? Weight: ???
Habitat: ??? Origin: India
Meaning: "Good fortune"

Rakashimi is the ancient Hindu goddess of wealth and consort of Vishnu, god of preservation, that originally became a part of the Buddhist religion. When shown in the company of Vishnu, she only has two arms, but when shown alone, she is drawn with four arms and sometimes with a red lotus flower.
