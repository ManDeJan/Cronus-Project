Sitanel (Satan)

Height: ??? Weight: ???
Habitat: Hell Origin: Macedonia
Meaning: Sitan (honest) + el (god)

Also called Satan, Sitanel is the famous angel who planned to rebel against God, but was cast out of heaven. Thereafter, Satan created a second "heaven" underground for those of evil persuasion. When Satan was preached about by one Christian sect, it became more popular and widely known.
