Slime

Height: 50 cm Weight: ???
Habitat: All Origin: America
Meaning: English "slime"

Slimes have become the quintessential weak monster in RPGs, both table-top and video. Slimes have an amorphous form and are able to wrap themselves around their opponents, suffocating them. The slime used in games is patterned after an amoeba-like creature that lived in the sea before animals inhabited the land.
