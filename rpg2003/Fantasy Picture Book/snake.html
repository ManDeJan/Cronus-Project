Snake

Length: 50 cm - 5 meters Weight: 1 - 5 lbs.
Habitat: Various Origin: ???
Meaning: English "snake"

Two types of snakes generally appear in games, giant snakes that attack with sheer size and cobra-like snakes such as the anaconda that attack using poison. Those snakes that poison usually cause bleeding under the skin, paraysis, or even death. Most snakes do not attack humans unless threatened, preferring to eat small animals or insects instead.
